<span data-ttu-id="0b0a6-103">Une instruction `Continue` doit être suivie de `Do`, `For`ou `While`, selon que l’instruction `Continue` apparaît dans une boucle `Do...Loop` , `For...Next` ou `While...End While` .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0b0a6-103">A `Continue` statement must be followed by `Do`, `For`, or `While`, depending on if the `Continue` statement appears within a `Do...Loop` loop, `For...Next` loop, or `While...End While` loop.</span></span>  
  
 <span data-ttu-id="0b0a6-104">**ID d’erreur :** BC30781</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0b0a6-104">**Error ID:** BC30781</span></span>
